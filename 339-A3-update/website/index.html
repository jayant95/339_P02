RECENT NEWS

Author: Nathan Drake

Date: 24/10/2018

We are pleased to introduce our new Stratis™ sails line. Sails are pre-impregnated fiber technology provides unmatched flexibility in fiber orientation, accuracy of placement and superior lamination. All fibers are load bearing, converging to every load point. Producing stronger, lighter sails that are much smoother when set, hold their shape better with significantly less stretch, and last longer than conventional sails.
